About Us

Mealshare idea began when its Founders, worried about the problem of food waste, decided to come up with a solution, taking advantage of both food that is still in perfect conditions and food that has second opportunity to be used in other fields.

Our Mision

Our mission is to manage food waste efficiently by connecting companies with surplus food and providing sustainable solutions. We work with supermarkets, restaurants, hotels, and the agricultural sector to ensure responsible use of food unfit for human consumption, benefiting vulnerable sectors.

Our Vision

Our vision is a world with minimal food waste, where every food resource is used responsibly and beneficially for all communities. .
